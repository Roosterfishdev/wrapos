FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need technical knowledge to use this?+
No. Setup is done through a simple dashboard. Embedding only requires copying and pasting a single script tag into your site.
Can I control pricing?+
Yes. All pricing logic is fully configurable. Set rates by vehicle type, wrap coverage area, and finish type — update anytime from your dashboard.
Does it work on my website?+
Yes. WrapOS works on any platform that supports script embeds — WordPress, Webflow, Shopify, Squarespace, and custom-built sites.
Can customers upload their own car?+
Yes. Image upload and automatic background removal are built into the experience — no extra tools or steps needed.
How accurate are the previews?+
Instant previews are fast and visually convincing for most customers. AI-enhanced mode improves realism further by simulating material properties, lighting, and reflections.
What happens after a quote is submitted?+
You receive the full submission in your lead dashboard — including customer info, selected wrap options, estimated pricing, and a visual of the configured wrap.
